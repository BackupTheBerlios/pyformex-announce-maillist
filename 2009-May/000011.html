Hi pyFormex users,

You are welcome on the next pyFormex user meeting at IBiTech on
Monday 11 May at 14.00h.

Among the topics to be discussed are some of the latest developments
- pyFormex USB stick
- BuMPix netboot server
- installing external software (gts, gl2ps, tetgen, calpy, admesh, units)
- exporting images using gl2ps
- 2D Text rendering (GLUT vs. QT)
- 3D font manipulations using FTGL
- (rectangle) zooming and mouse key bindings
- OpenGL3 and consequences for the future of pyFormex
- local repositories
- DXF format exporter
- using projects

Gianluca will also show us his user-friendly hex meshing from stl of 
arterial bifurcations.

Everybody welcome.
BTW, minutes of the last two meetings will soon appear on the website.

And for those wondering what this 1000 in the subject lines means: we 
just reached the 1000-th revision of pyFormex! Upgrade, and you will 
find a hidden Easter Egg.

Benedict
